    # Simple Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 3⁄4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until combined.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and grease with butter.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup or toppings of choice.
